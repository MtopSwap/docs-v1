# Team

## About the Founder

MtopSwap was founded by David in February 2022. Previously a professional piano player & graphic designer, he began trading on DeFi ecosystems in 2020 & built a network of trusted friends.  

The project was conceived after noticing frustrating user experiences & fragmentation of ecosystems & DEXes, with the goal of creating a one-stop-shop for all things DeFi. 

## About the Team

The MtopSwap team, community managers, & mods are a network of blockchain enthusiasts & friends who have worked on various projects together.  They are of various backgrounds - community leaders & validators, IT architectural engineers, graphic designers, full-stack developers, system & process engineers, & beyond.
